As a Criminologist I am thrilled to connect people with a fervent enthusiasm for the convergence of disciplines that define our modern age.

My journey in understanding the intricate dance between criminal behavior and the digital realm has led me to explore the dynamic intersections of computer technology.

Crime has evolved, weaving its narrative through the intricate threads of our interconnected world. My passion lies in deciphering these complexities, unraveling the digital fingerprints left by those who navigate the shadows.
